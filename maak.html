Z Splash White Water Rafting

Z Splash White Water Rafting is a company founded by Vuyelwa Macanda in 2025. The mission of the company is to provide affordable white water rafting experiences in South Africa. With over 10 years of experience Vuyelwa Macanda has created the best enviroment for those who are going for the first time and those with experience. With 5 different adventures to explore, our company also accomodates tourists as our staff includes french, spanish, portuguese and local language speakers, ensuring clear communication for your safety and pleasure during your adventure.

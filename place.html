About my country, South Africa

South Africa is a country located at the southern tip of the African continent, it is bordered by Namibia, Botswana, Zimbabwe, Mozambique, Swaziland, and Lesotho. South Africa is also known as the rainbow nation and has 11 official languages making it rich in cluture and diversity. South Africa has a diverse wildlife with elephants, lions, rhinos, and leopards
